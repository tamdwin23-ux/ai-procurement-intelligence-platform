# 🛒 AI Autonomous Procurement Intelligence Platform

> An enterprise AI procurement platform that automates purchase request analysis, vendor evaluation, supplier recommendations, approval workflows, and procurement intelligence using **n8n, OpenAI, Supabase, PostgreSQL, Make, and Zapier**.

---

# 🚀 Business Problem

Procurement teams often spend hours reviewing purchase requests, comparing vendor quotations, checking budgets, identifying duplicate requests, and routing approvals.

Manual procurement processes can result in:

- Slow purchasing cycles
- Duplicate purchases
- Higher procurement costs
- Limited vendor visibility
- Approval delays
- Budget overruns
- Human error

---

# 💡 Solution

This AI-powered Procurement Intelligence Platform automates procurement operations by analyzing purchase requests, evaluating suppliers, checking company policies, detecting duplicate purchases, and routing approvals to the appropriate stakeholders.

The system enables procurement teams to make faster, smarter, and more cost-effective purchasing decisions.
